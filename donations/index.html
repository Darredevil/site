Donations

You can make a donation anytime in ROSEdu's account: RO98BTRL04501205R50291XX, Banca Transilvania S.A. .

You can also donate 2% from your state tax as a individual, by filling one of the forms:

  • for the year 2015 use form 230 - the deadline is 25th of May 2016.
  • for the year 2016 use form 230 - the deadline is 25th of May 2017.

If you represent a legal entity you can donate 20% by filling one of the forms:

  • for the year 2015 form 200 - the deadline is 25th of May 2016.
  • for the year 2016 form 200 - the deadline is 31st of December 2016.

The form has to be sent to the ANAF agency from your jurisdiction, more info is found after each form.

Why?

In order to continually develop our projects, ROSEdu needs an annual budget to cover the costs of each program. By making a donation, you can help us in our mission of bringing open-source to Romanian students.
